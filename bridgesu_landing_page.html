Why we built this.

Bridge@SU was started by a first-generation college student at Syracuse University who kept hearing the same thing from high schoolers: "I didn't know that was an option."

SU students know things guidance counselors don't always have time to share. We built Bridge@SU to close that gap — one email, one video, one conversation at a time.

  • Knowledge is a right, not a privilege. Every student deserves access to the same information.
  • Peer advice lands differently. We've been exactly where you are.
  • Community is the whole point. You're not navigating this alone.
"I had no idea what FAFSA was until junior year. Nobody in my family had gone through the college application process, so I was figuring everything out alone. I started Bridge@SU because no high schooler should have to feel that lost navigating something this important."
