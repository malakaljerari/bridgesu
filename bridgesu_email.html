3 things to do this month
  • 1
    Make a free Common App account this summer, even if you're only a sophomore. Get familiar with what it asks before you actually need it.
  • 2
    Email one teacher or counselor this week and ask: "What's one scholarship or program you'd recommend for a student like me?" Most people love being asked.
  • 3
    FAFSA opens October 1st for seniors every year. It's free and required for almost all financial aid. Put it on your calendar now, before senior year gets busy.
